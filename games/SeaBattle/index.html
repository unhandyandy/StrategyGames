Sea Battle





Messages.


This is a modern version of Hnefatafl which is both simple and well balanced. White's goal is to get his king safely to edge of the board; Black's goal is to capture White's king. All pieces move like chess rooks. All pieces except the king are captured custodian-wise: by surrounding a single piece on two opposite sides, either horizontally or vertically. To capture the king it must be surrounded on all four sides. That's it.
